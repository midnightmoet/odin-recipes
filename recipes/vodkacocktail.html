Pineapple Screwdriver

Pineapple Screwdriver cocktails are super simple to make! Easy to mix up by the glass or the pitcher with pineapple juice, vodka, and a splash of orange juice. This is a great cocktail for a crowd!

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Yeild: 2

Serving size:1

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces pineapple juice
  • 6 ounces orange juice
  • 4 ounces vodka (your choice on the brand)

    • Optional Garnishes:

    • Fresh mint
    • Maraschino cherries
    • Pineapple slices
    • Orange slices / peel
    • Ice

Directions

  1. Mix pineapple juice, orange juice and vodka together.
  2. Fill glass with ice and top with pineapple screwdriver mixture.
  3. Garnish with mint, cherries, orange peel or pineapple, if desired.
