Sex On The Beach

Combine vodka with peach schnapps and cranberry juice to make a classic sex on the beach cocktail. Garnish with cocktail cherries and orange slices

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Serves:2

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 50ml vodka
  • 25ml peach schnapps
  • 2 oranges, juiced, plus 2 slices to garnish
  • 50ml cranberry juice

    • Optional Ingredients:

    • glace cherries, to garnish

Directions

  1. Fill two tall glasses with ice cubes. Pour the vodka, peach schnapps and fruit juices into a large jug and stir.
  2. Divide the mixture between the two glasses and stir gently to combine. Garnish with the cocktail cherries and orange slices.
