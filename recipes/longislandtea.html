Long Island Tea

One thing must be cleared up about the Long Island Iced Tea straight away: It is living a lie. For starters, it's not entirely clear that it's actually from Long Island. That's debatable. But, folks, it is most assuredly not tea. You will not find a drop of tea in this cocktail. But don't hold it against it. Read on to learn how to make this 5-star recipe for Long Island Iced Tea.

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Serving:1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 fluid ounce vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce rum
  • 1/2 fluid ounce gin
  • 1/2 fluid ounce tequila
  • 1/2 fluid ounce triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)
  • 1 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix
  • 1 fluid ounce cola
  • 1 slice lemon

Directions

  1. Fill shaker with ice.
  2. Pour vodka, run, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sour mix over ice.
  3. Cover and shake.
  4. Pour cocktail into a Collins or hurricane glass.
  5. Top off with splash of cola for color.
  6. Garnish with lemon slice.
