Smoothie

I have been experimenting with different flavor combinations, and I have to say, this tropical smoothie recipe is our new favorite! It is full of tropical fruit with some coconut milk for a creamy texture and richer flavor. You can get all fancy with the garnishes like I did here, or simply serve it as is; either way it is sure to be a hit!

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 1 minutes

Serves:2

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup canned coconut milk light is ok
  • 1 large banana sliced. You can freeze your banana before you add it to the blender for an even thicker smoothie.
  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 1 cup frozen pineapple

    • Optional Variations:

    • Liquids: Instead of pineapple juice, try coconut water, mango nectar or pineapple-orange-banana juice.
    • Fruit: You can add other fruits to the mix such as kiwi, papaya, or even strawberries.
    • Add Ins: Give your smoothie a boost of nutrition by adding spinach, kale, protein powder, flax seeds or chia seeds.
    • Optional garnishes: Maraschino cherries, lime slices, mint sprigs, pineapple wedges

Directions

  1. Place the pineapple juice, coconut milk, banana, mango and pineapple in a blender; blend until completely smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into 2 glasses and add optional garnishes if desired. Serve immediately.
