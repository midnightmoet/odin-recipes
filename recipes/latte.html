White Chocolate Latte

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Servings:2

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 TBSP heavy cream
  • 1/8 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 TBSP white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brewed espresso
  • 1/4 cup white chocolate chips, chopped

    • Optional Ingredients:

    • 2-3 crushed peppermint candies

Directions

  1. Combine the milk and cream in a sauce pan, and whisk over high heat until hot and frothy.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla and sugar
  3. Whisk together hot espresso and white chocolate chips in a mug until smooth. (If making 2, pour half into another mug.)
  4. Top with frothy hot milk and stir to blend in the flavoring
  5. Top with crushed peppermint candies, if desired
