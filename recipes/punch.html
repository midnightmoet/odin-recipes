Pimm's Punch

Pimm’s punch is the perfect big batch punch recipe for your parties. Full of citrus goodness, it tastes great and is so easy to make. Let’s start at the beginning. What exactly is Pimm’s? Pimm’s is a British liqueur made from a liquor base with citrus and herbs.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Serves:3-4

Ingredients

  • 1 blood orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 1 lb whole strawberries
  • 1 bunch fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup of lemon-lime soda or ginger ale
  • 4 1/2 cups Sparkling Ice Strawberry Lemonade

    • Items needed:

    • Pitcher or Punch Bowl
    • Chef's Knife
    • Cutting Board
    • Bar Spoon
    • Optional to make adult beverage add: 1 750-ml bottle Pimm’s No. 1 AND 1 cup of gin

Directions

  1. In a large punch bowl, combine the blood oranges, lemons, cucumbers, strawberries, and half of the mint.
  2. Pour in the Pimm’s, gin, and Sparkling Ice Strawberry Lemonade, stir until combined. OR
  3. Pour in the Sparkling Ice Strawberry Lemonade, lemon-lime OR ginger ale, stir until combined.
  4. Fill glasses with ice and pour or ladle in the punch, garnish with the remaining mint and serve.
